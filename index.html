Our Story

Born from Faith,
Built on Prayer

Christ's House of Worship was born out of a deep spiritual calling. Founded by Pastor Patra John Calvin, the church began its journey in 2015 in Annavarapadu, Ongole — a small gathering of believers with a great vision.

Through years of faithful ministry, the congregation grew steadily. In 2022, God opened the doors to our own church building in Cloughpet, Ongole — a landmark moment in the church's history and a testament to His faithfulness.

Today, CHOW stands as a vibrant community of believers committed to worship, discipleship, and community outreach across Ongole and beyond.

Founding & Senior Pastor

Patra John Calvin

Senior Pastor · Christ's House of Worship
"Our calling is not just to build a church building — it is to build lives that glorify God in every season."

Pastor Patra John Calvin founded Christ's House of Worship in 2015 with a heartfelt vision to establish a place where every person — regardless of background — could encounter the living God.

His ministry is characterized by deep prayer, expository preaching, and a passion for reaching the unreached in Ongole's tier-2 community. Under his leadership the church has grown from a handful of families to a thriving congregation with its own building and multiple active ministries.
